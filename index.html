ABOUT LENE RAY

I'm Lene Ray, a professional photographer and videographer with over 8 years of experience capturing moments that matter. My love for photography began in childhood, always with a camera in hand, and eventually grew into a career that has taken me from local projects to national recognition.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working on major campaigns for BID companies, with my films featured in cinemas, magazines, and the press. One of the music videos I directed even reached No.1 on the iTunes music video chart and was later featured on BBC News.

My portfolio includes collaborations with a wide range of musicians and bands, shooting everything from album covers to promotional posters. I've covered festivals and live shows for artists like Olly Murs, Happy Mondays, Gabrielle, Jools Holland, Scouting for Girls, and The Hoosiers. My reviews and photography are regularly published in The Bristol Gig Guide, and my work has also appeared in Festival Brides, Exeter Living, Devon Live, BBC, Brides, God Is in the TV Zine, and even across Stagecoach buses throughout the South West.

I'm also passionate about using creativity for good. A recent artist collaboration raised over Â£500 for the mental health charity MIND.

Whether I'm working from my home studio, in professional spaces, or on location using natural light and creative props, I bring a relaxed and personal approach to every shoot. I also runMoments to Media, my sister business focused on capturing weddings and turning them into timeless, heartfelt films.

At the core of everything I do is a deep love for storytelling. Whether through still images or moving visuals and a commitment to making people feel seen, celebrated, and connected.
